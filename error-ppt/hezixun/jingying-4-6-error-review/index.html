阅读理解 · 第 35 题

📖 原文语境

There are lots of things that are good for your brain. Reading, for example, helps your brain grow and work better. Solving puzzles can help too. Scientists now find out another way to improve brain health: exercising.
...
Exercise is also good for patients with Alzheimer's disease, which harms the brain and causes memory loss.

What's the best title for the text?

A. Exercise is good for health
B. The importance of a healthy brain
C. Exercise helps bring health to the brains
D. Different ways of keeping the brains healthy
✅ 正确答案：C

📝 考点

主旨大意题（文章标题归纳）

📋 解析

本文开篇就点明主题"Scientists now find out another way to improve brain health: exercising."，然后从婴儿、儿童、成年人三个角度分别说明了锻炼对大脑健康的好处，整篇文章都围绕"锻炼有益于大脑健康"展开，所以C选项正确。

❌ 错因分析

学生误选D，没有抓住文章核心。本文虽然开头提到了阅读、拼图等多种保持大脑健康的方法，但重点在于介绍"锻炼"这一种方法，而不是"多种不同方法"，所以D不准确。

短文填空 · 第 56 题

Hello, I am David. I live in South Africa. My grandmother always tells me, "If you lie down with dogs, you will get up with fleas." She also says that there are many ___56___ (kind) of people in the world, and I should 57. follow (follow) the good ones.

提示词：kind

✅ 正确答案：kinds

📝 考点

名词复数形式与固定搭配

📋 解析

空格前面有形容词many修饰，many后面必须接可数名词的复数形式，所以kind要变成kinds。"many kinds of"表示"许多种"，修饰后面的people。

🔍 易混辨析

kind of 表示"有点儿"，是固定短语，后面接形容词修饰程度，如: "He is kind of tall." 他有点儿高。
kinds of 后面接名词复数，表示"各种各样的"。本题中many后需要接复数名词kinds。

❌ 错因分析

学生对many修饰可数名词复数这一语法规则掌握不牢，或混淆了kind of与kinds of的用法。

完形填空 · 第 11 题

As a Changchun student, I always look forward to Jilin's snow vacation—it s not just a break, but a chance to enjoy winter's magic. This year's vacation left a deep ___11___ in my heart.

A. mark
B. message
C. sign
D. symbol
✅ 正确答案：A

📝 考点

名词词义辨析与固定搭配

📋 解析

"leave a deep mark in one's heart" 是固定搭配，表示"在某人心中留下深刻的印记/痕迹"。mark表示"痕迹、印记"，符合假期留下深刻记忆的语境；sign主要指"符号、标牌、迹象"，不符合此处搭配。

❌ 错因分析

学生未能准确区分mark和sign的词义差别，对固定搭配不熟悉。

I finally skied down the slopes—wind blowing ___15___ my face, it felt like flying! My little sister played in the snow with Mom, and their laughter could be heard far away.

阅读理解 · 第 27 题

📖 原文语境

...
Eat well
Our cookery course will show you how to make the most delicious meals. Get healthier by developing your cooking skills every Thursday and Friday evening in our school dining hall. The course is from 17:00 to 18:30. You can have a chance to taste the food your classmates make.
...

How many times can students take the cookery course every month?

A. Over ten times.
B. Eight to ten times.
C. Seven times in total.
D. No more than eight times.
✅ 正确答案：B

📝 考点

细节推理计算题

📋 解析

文中说课程在every Thursday and Friday evening（每周四和周五晚上），一个月大约有4周，4 × 2 = 8次，最多可能有5周就是10次，所以是"八到十次"eight to ten times。

❌ 错因分析

学生计算错误，未能根据每周次数推算每月大约次数。一个月4-5周，每周两次就是8-10次。
